### Title
**The Disappearing Act â€” Ice Melts, But the Mass Never Leaves**

### Overview
Students model the apparent "disappearing act" when ice melts â€” discovering that the mass doesn't vanish, it just changes form. By building a cause-and-effect model showing how temperature shifts matter between solid and liquid states while keeping total mass constant, students internalize the law of conservation of matter. The puzzle hook ("where did it go?") drives curiosity and the model provides the satisfying answer through simulation.

### Learning Objectives

- Model how temperature shifts matter between solid and liquid form
- Predict what happens to each state of matter as temperature changes
- Apply conservation of matter to explain everyday physical changes
