â€¢ "Bigger magnets are always stronger"

ANSWER KEY:
Big Question: How can a force you can't see, smell, or touch move objects and transfer energy?
Answer: Invisible electromagnetic forces work because moving electric charges (current) create magnetic fields around them. By wrapping wire into coils and increasing current, we concentrate this invisible force into something strong enough to lift cars in junkyards â€” all from a force you can't see!
